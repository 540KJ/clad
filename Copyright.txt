                 RULES OF CONTRIBUTION AND COPYRIGHT OWNERSHIP

 Everyone is permitted to copy and distribute verbatim copies of this document, 
 but changing it is not allowed.

0. LICENSE
  License.txt describes clad as an open source project. By contributing to  the
 project you help not only clad to evolve but to develop the open source
 community. 

1. COPYRIGHT OF CONTRIBUTIONS
  Every contribution is very welcome and its copyright and any other related 
 rights become exclusive ownership of clad Founders (see Readme.md).

2. QUALITY OF CONTRIBUTIONS
  In order for a contribution to be accepted it has to obey the previously 
established rules for contribution acceptance in clad's work flow and rules.
